Stop lying to yourself - your budgeting skills are bad. You tell yourself that you excel at predicting all future expenses but when the time comes you succumb to a completely unnecessary Iced Capp.

Stop trying to predict the future and embrace a totally random lifestyle. Using BudgetME, you do not have to guess what you will be expending for the forseeable future; let a random algorithm dictate what your consumption will look like for the next week or month. You will save lots of money.*

* BudgetME is a random algorithm so you might spend all of your budget on yo-yo strings. You will likely stay within budget but that does not mean it is spent wisely. It does grant you a wild lifestyle as you will never experience routine again.
